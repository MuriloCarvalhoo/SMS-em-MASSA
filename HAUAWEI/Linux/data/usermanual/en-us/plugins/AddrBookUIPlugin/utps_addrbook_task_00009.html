Adding a Contact

This topic describes how to add a contact to a group.

Procedure

  1. Click .
  2. Choose the method to add the contact.
    Option Description

    To directly add a contact in a group
    1. Select the group in the navigation tree.
    2. Click .
    3. Enter the contact information to create a contact.

    To add a contact from Local to another group
    1. Select one or more contacts in Local.
    2. Drag the selected contact(s) to another group.
    NOTE:
    When you drag the selected contact(s), press Ctrl/Command to copy the contact(s) to another group.
