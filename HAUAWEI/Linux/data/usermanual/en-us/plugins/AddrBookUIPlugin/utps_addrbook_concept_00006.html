Group Management

You can assign the contacts to different groups on the Mobile Partner.

  • Creating a Group
    This topic describes how to create a group.
  • Changing a Group Name
    This topic describes how to change a group name for clearly classifying the contact groups.
  • Adding a Contact
    This topic describes how to add a contact to a group.
  • Removing a Contact
    This topic describes how to remove a contact from a group. The contact is removed from the group but not deleted.
  • Deleting a Group
    This topic describes how to delete a contact group. Contacts belong to the deleted group are automatically stored in Local.
